how to be successful?

I’ll come straight to the point: there are only a few things that you can do that will massively impact your chances of being successful. Step 1: Improving Focus Extreme Self-Discipline: First, you need to bring extreme self-discipline into all parts of your life. In a way, you’ll have to boost your morale in every part of life. This is important because we often feel that for our special projects, like a startup or a book, we can increase our focus. But actually, how you live your hours is how you live your day, and how you live your day is how you live your life.
